Investor Relations

Paytm is a well-known Indian e-commerce payment system and financial technology company. It was founded in 2010 by Vijay Shekhar Sharma and is headquartered in Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. Paytm started as a mobile recharge and utility bill payment platform and has since evolved into a diverse financial services platform.
